RFE Review

Every RFE is automatically assessed daily at 4:00 AM UTC. A failing score blocks the RFE from progressing.

How it works

RFE Ingestion
Pipeline fetches all RFEs from Jira for assessment
AI Assessment
Each RFE is scored independently against the quality rubric
Auto-Revision
Failing RFEs are automatically improved where possible
Feasibility Check
Technical feasibility is evaluated and labeled on the Jira ticket
Human Review
RFEs that can't be auto-fixed are flagged for PM action

What you need to do

  • Write your business justification in the Description field — that's what the AI reads
  • Watch for the needs-attention label on your Jira tickets
  • If your RFE fails, edit the ticket — it'll be re-assessed on the next daily run
  • Focus on avoiding zero scores — any zero is an automatic fail regardless of total

Scoring quick reference

Pass: ≥ 7/10 total AND no zeros on any criterion

WHAT 0-2
Is the customer need clearly described?
Zero = "Unclear Outcome"
WHY 0-2
Is there business justification?
Named customers/revenue → 2 · Generic segments → 1 · Nothing → 0 "WHY Void"
HOW 0-2
Does it leave architecture to engineering?
Referencing platform technologies is fine · Zero = "Architecture Prescription"
TASK 0-2
Is this a business need, not a chore?
Zero = "Task Masquerade"
SIZE 0-2
Does it map to roughly one strategy feature?
Zero = "Kitchen-Sink Scope"

Jira Labels Reference
autofix-rubric-pass RFE passed quality scoring
needs-attention Automation couldn't resolve all issues — you need to act
auto-revised Content was improved by automation
auto-created RFE was created by the pipeline
feasibility-pass Technically feasible
feasibility-fail Technically infeasible — review and revise
feasibility-unknown Feasibility couldn't be determined
split-original This RFE was decomposed into smaller RFEs
split-result This RFE was produced by splitting another
